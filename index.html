Minesweeper

How to Play

1.Click on the cells to reveal them and avoid mines.

2.Numbers in revealed cells show how many mines are adjacent to that cell.

3.Right-click to flag a cell as a mine to mark it.

4.Be cautious and use the numbers to deduce where the mines might be!

Difficulty Levels:

  • Easy: 9×9 grid with 20 mines
  • Medium: 10×12 grid with 40 mines
  • Hard: 12×14 grid with 80 mines
